Health Risks of Obesity

1. Cardiovascular Disease

Definition: Cardiovascular disease refers to disorders of the heart and blood vessels, including coronary artery disease, which can lead to heart attack or stroke.

Recommendations

General Recommendations:

  • Aim to lose 5–10% of your body weight to significantly reduce risk.
  • Regularly monitor blood pressure and cholesterol levels.

Food Recommendation:

  • Eat heart-healthy foods such as whole grains, lean proteins, and leafy greens.
  • Avoid trans fats and sugary drinks.

Exercise Recommendation:

  • Engage in at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity per week.
  • Incorporate strength training exercises 2–3 times a week.
